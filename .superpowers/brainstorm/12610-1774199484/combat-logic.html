Combat & Enemy Logic

Section 2 of 3 — How the fight resolves and how the enemy behaves

Combat Resolution (when timer hits 0)

Attack vs Shield

Player Attack − Enemy Shield = damage to Enemy HP
Enemy Attack − Player Shield = damage to Player HP
Minimum 0 (can't heal opponent)

Magic vs Aura

Player Magic − Enemy Aura = damage to Enemy HP
Enemy Magic − Player Aura = damage to Player HP
Shield does NOT block Magic. Aura does NOT block Attack.

Example Round
Player
Attack: 5  Shield: 3
Magic: 4  Aura: 1
Enemy
Attack: 3  Shield: 2
Magic: 6  Aura: 0
Enemy takes: (5−2) + (4−0) = 7 damage
Player takes: (3−3) + (6−1) = 5 damage

Enemy AI (useEnemyAI hook)

The enemy fills stats on a tick interval (every ~3–4 seconds). Each tick it picks a random stat and adds 1 point — the player can see the enemy bar fill in real-time and react.

Slow Potion effect

Tick interval doubles for 15 seconds. Visual indicator on enemy side shows they are slowed.

Heal Potion effect

Instantly adds +5 to Player HP (capped at 20). Can only be used during Setup phase.

Math Problem Flow

  1. Player taps Attack / Shield / Magic / Aura button → question appears in the panel to the right of the button
  2. Player uses NumPad to type answer and hits ✓
  3. Correct → stat +1, new question ready, button lights up green briefly
  4. Wrong → input clears, button shakes red, same question stays (no penalty beyond time lost)
  5. Potion tap → same flow but stat +3 on correct answer, potion is consumed regardless of answer

Only one question active at a time. Tapping a different button mid-answer cancels the current question with no penalty.
